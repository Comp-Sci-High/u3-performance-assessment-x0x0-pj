About

Versace is an Italian luxury fashion house known for its bold, glamorous designs, innovative Italian artisanship, and association with celebrities. The current owner is Dontalella Versace, but the company was founded by Gianni Versace back in 1978. Famous celebrities like Anne Hathaway, Hwang Hyunjin, and Sabrina Carpenter are the top models/ambassedors.
